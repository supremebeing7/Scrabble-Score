Scrabble-Score
==============

Takes a word and returns the Scrabble score for it. Here are the values of letters in Scrabble:  

    A, E, I, O, U, L, N, R, S, T       1
    D, G                               2
    B, C, M, P                         3
    F, H, V, W, Y                      4
    K                                  5
    J, X                               8
    Q, Z                               10
